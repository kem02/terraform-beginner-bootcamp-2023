Pumpkin Pie

  • 2 cups of canned pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground nutmeg
  • 1/8 teaspoon of ground cloves
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup of evaporated milk
  • 1 pre-made pie crust (9-inch)
  1. Preparation:
    • Preheat your oven to 425째F (220째C).
    • Place the pie crust into a 9-inch pie dish and set aside.
  2. Filling:

    • Beat in the eggs, one at a time, ensuring they are well incorporated.
    • Gradually stir in the evaporated milk until the mixture is smooth.
  3. Baking:
    • Pour the filling into the prepared pie crust.
    • Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes.
    • Reduce the oven temperature to 350째F (175째C) and continue baking for another 40 to 50 minutes, or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean.
  4. Cooling & Serving:
    • Allow the pie to cool on a wire rack for 2 hours.
    • You can serve it chilled or at room temperature, with a dollop of whipped cream if desired.
